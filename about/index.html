About

This little place on the internet is about myself. Click on the links for magic!!! 🤯

My core skills are in Web Development and Programming. My misadventure with formal education and my passion for helping people and making a change in the world around me made me want to study Humanities in school. I had Political Science, Mathematics, Economics, History, English and Computer Science(6th Subject) as my subjects in school. Grandma and society weren't pleased. But neither was I with studying Science subjects over subjects where I learn about how our society works. After her initial heartbreak my Grandma told me that "the heart wants what the heart wants". I had no idea what it was at the time. But I knew it wasn't Chemistry or JEE. I discovered the beautiful world of Open Source 4 years back and haven't stopped contributing since. After going through 11 years of the Indian Education System and teaching in one of the better Government Schools of the state for 3 months, I have a burning desire to make education better.
